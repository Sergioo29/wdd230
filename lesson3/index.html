Site Purpose

As students, we usually live in student apartment complexes. Since the rent prices are subject to change at any time, most students find themselves moving from one apartment to another pretty often. Moving that often makes it more difficult to keep some belongings. A student may want to get rid of some items, but just giving it away does not seem too advantageous. Also, students studying business and other simillar fields may want to start right away, but the required resources and complexity of the proccess may drive them away. With that in mind, I created this chamber of commerce for Rexburg citizens, targeting BYUI students. In this website, any person or group will be able to search for items or services as well as post their own. The end goal is to have a user-friendly, student drived website where anyone will feel confortable sharing their business ideas. The website will also provide a "recruiting" option, where the owner of the idea may request help from other fellow students.
