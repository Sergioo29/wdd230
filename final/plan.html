Site Name

A Sweet Suite



Domain

asweetsuite.com



Site Purpose

This website will serve and a mock site for a ficticious company that serves lodging for people that are visiting various places around The United States of America just to see The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Saints church temple there. The site will have a temple finder with the place of the lodge as well as pictures of it. There will also be a services page with all the amenities of the company, with both free as part of the reservation and paid services that the company can offer. We want this site to have a cozy feeling, friendly and simple layout, and appealing images for the customers to feel at ease when making a reservation.



Target Audience

The site will be more centralized in reaching members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. With that being said, we will use some words and images that may be confusing for non-members. Still, we will try to make the website friendly for non-members that may be interested in visiting the outter parts of the temples or other areas that are available to them, like the open houses that happen periodically and also the Visitors Center.



Scenarios

  • A member from Brazil is traveling to Utah to visit the Idaho Falls's temple. She does not know anyone that lives near the temple to stay for the night, and the hotels in Trivago are too expensive. This website will help to find affordable places fit for her.
  • A non-member wants to visit the Salt Lake temple, he wants to have a full experience of the Mormon culture. This website will offer services like missionary teaching along with the lodging.
